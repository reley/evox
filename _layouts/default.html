eVOX

A hybrid solution, where brokers and the electronic system exist side by side. Allowing participants to enjoy the efficiencies of electronic trade negotiation while also having the option of discussing bespoke products with brokers.
A true multi broker market place.

Trading

The platform preserves anonymity throughout the community, offering trading models for matched principal and introducing broker. Feedback from the community instigated features such as participant restricted and public for orders. In short, you can either define a who you can negotiate and trade with per order, if you have opted to restrict an order being seen by a member of the eVOX community, this action will be unknown.

We believe our true multi-broker facilitation to be a unique selling point. Participants can “tag” brokers whom they would like to be considered to complete the trade. This single pane of glass approach has received great community feedback and is some we are proud to provide.
